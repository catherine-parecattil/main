    About IEDC

    Discover Our Mission & Vision

    The Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC) at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham Kochi is a dynamic hub designed to cultivate and empower the next generation of innovators and entrepreneurs. We provide a state-of-the-art ecosystem where students can transform groundbreaking ideas into tangible projects and viable startups.

    Our mission is to foster a culture of creativity, problem-solving, and risk-taking. Through mentorship, workshops, access to resources, and collaborative spaces, IEDC equips aspiring minds with the tools and knowledge needed to navigate the complexities of the modern tech and business landscape. We are building a future where innovation knows no bounds.

    Our Flagship Innovation Contest

    The Annual Tech Fest at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, Kochi is a premier event organized by the Department of Computer Science & Information Technology in collaboration with the Computer Society of India (CSI), Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre (IEDC), Software Innovators' Club (SIC), and Institution's Innovation Council (IIC).

    This fest brings together students, industry experts, and innovators to showcase cutting-edge technology and ideas. The event fosters creativity, innovation, and collaboration, serving as a platform for participants to explore advancements in the tech world.
